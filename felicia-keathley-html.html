Felicia Keathley - Age, Family, Bio

Health and fitness Instagram star who uses her page to track her weight loss journey, aided along the way by WW (the new name of the Weight Watchers company). She uploads photos of her daily meals, work outs and inspiration stories. She has 470,000 followers on Instagram. 

Before Fame

Her first Instagram post was on May 31 2017 and was a photo of her son. She was one week into her WW journey and had lost 8.6 pounds. 

Trivia

Her story was featured on the official WW page and on the news and entertainment website UniLad. 

Family Life

She and her husband have been together since they were in middle school. They have a son named Weston, born 2016. 

Associated With

Kristine Steele and DollysWholeLife also have inspirational weight loss stories achieved with the help of Weight Watchers.
