It appears there may be a lot more than fans know about the working relationship between Nollywood actress Funke Akindele-Bello and colleague, Baba Tee.

Just recently, the comic actor took to his official page on Instagram with an appreciation post dedicated to the Omo Ghetto actress.

Baba Tee in his post described the actress as “a star-maker of our generation” while thanking her for the role she has played in his career.

The actor listed movie productions like Omo Ghetto, My Siblings and I, Jenifa among others that he has been given the opportunity to be a part of.

Baba Tee concluded his post to the actress by describing her as his hero.
