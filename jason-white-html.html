Jason White - Age, Family, Bio

Touring guitarist with the punk rock group Green Day as well as a guitarist and vocalist with the group Pinhead Gunpowder. In Green Day, he is joined by drummer Tre Cool, bassist Mike Dirnt, and singer/guitarist Billie Joe Armstrong. 

Before Fame

He was a last-minute stand-in for a Bay Area punk band called Monsula when Green Day was touring through Little Rock.

Trivia

He has been with Green Day in an unofficial capacity since 1999; finally, in 2012, they made him an official member.

Family Life

He married his wife Janna Rollins in 2005. They have a child named Sonny.

Associated With

Mike Dirnt co-founded Green Day and convinced White to join the band.
