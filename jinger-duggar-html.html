Jinger Duggar - Age, Family, Bio

One of 19 children of Jim Bob and Michelle Duggar featured on the reality series 19 Kids and Counting. She is the 6th oldest child and the 4th daughter of the family.  

Before Fame

Growing up she enjoyed photography, playing the piano, and shopping. 

Trivia

While her parents want her to remain close to home, she has aspirations to live in a big city. 

Family Life

She is from the small town of Tontitown, Arkansas. All of her siblings' names begin with the letter "J." In 2016, she married soccer player Jeremy Vuolo. She and Jeremy welcomed their first daughter, Felicity, in 2018. Their second daughter, Evangeline, was born in November 2020. 

Associated With

One of her older sisters is Jill Duggar.
