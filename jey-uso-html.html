Jey Uso - Age, Family, Bio

Tag team specialist who wrestled for the WWE as one half of the twin brother duo, The Usos. They are the first team to be Undisputed WWE Tag Team Champions after simultaneously holding the RAW and SmackDown titles. 

Before Fame

He made his professional debut in World Xtreme Wrestling in 2007.

Trivia

He and his brother were Tag Team Champions, while competing for Florida Championship Wrestling.

Family Life

He is the twin brother of Jimmy Uso and his brother Solo Sikoa are also wrestlers. His father is the famous wrestler Rikishi. He married Takecia Travis in 2015. They have two sons named Jaciyah and Jeyce.

Associated With

He has wrestled with his brother Jimmy Uso under the ring names The Usos, The Fatu Brothers, and The Fatu Twins.
