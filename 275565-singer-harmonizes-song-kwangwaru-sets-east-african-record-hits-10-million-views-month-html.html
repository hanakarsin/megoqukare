Singer Harmonize's song Kwangwaru sets new East African record, hits 10 million views in one month

- Harmonize expressed his joy on social media after his song hit the 10 million views mark in less than a month

- The song dubbed Kwangwaru became the first ever song in East Africa to achieve such a milestone

- The singer gave props to Diamond, claiming he played a big role in the success

There are songs, and there are hits.

Now, Harmonize's song Kwangwaru featuring his boss Diamond Platinumz definitely falls on the latter category.
