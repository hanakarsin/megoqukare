Umahi: We Remain Loyal To Our Party

Nigeria Governors elected on the platform of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) has stated that they remain loyal and committeed to strengthening the main opposition party.

They made this known during the ongoing 90th NEC meeting of the party holding at the party’s National Secretariat, Wadata House, Abuja.

Speaking on behalf of his colleagues, Chairman of the PDP Governors Forum, Aminu Tambuwal, stated that the party governors remain united .

He said, “All 15 of us are here present. We are individually committed to good governance in our various states to in order to make our party strong.

“This is a clear statement that we are united and working towards strengthening our party for the future.”

Naija News had reported earlier that the Ebonyi Governor announced his defection from PDP to All Progressives Congress (APC) while addressing journalists in Abakaliki, the Ebonyi State capital on Tuesday, November 17.

Governor Umahi said he moved to the ruling APC because of injustice meted out to the Southeast by his former political party, the PDP.

The Ebonyi Governor clarified that he has no deal with the APC or any member of the party with regards to the zoning of the presidential position to the southeast in 2023 as touted in some quarters.
