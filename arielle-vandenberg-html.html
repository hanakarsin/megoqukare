Arielle Vandenberg - Age, Family, Bio

Model and actress who had recurring roles on Meet the Browns and Greek and also made appearances on How I Met Your Mother and CSI: Miami. She has more than 1.4 million followers on Instagram.

Before Fame

She attended Fallbrook Union High School.

Trivia

She appeared in the spoof comedic film Epic Movie in 2007.

Family Life

In 2017, she began dating Matt Cutshall; the couple became engaged in September 2019. She previously dated actor Will Arnett and singer Alex Turner. 

Associated With

She appeared in the 2009 romance comedy The Ugly Truth with Katherine Heigl.
