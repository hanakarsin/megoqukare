Rich man buried in a lion-like coffin in Enugu (photos)

Wonders shall never end! Nigerians are creative no doubt and their creativity is now being extended to how their late family members are being buried.

According to a Facebook user, a rich man was buried with a lion like casket in Enugu state.

According to the eyewitness who shared photos from the burial on Facebook, he claimed that the late man was his good friend and that he could not believe his eyes when he saw the casket the man would be buried in. He also noted that he took time to ask the mortuary attendants why he was to be buried in a lion-like casket and they told him it was because the man was rich before his death. He emphatically noted that the burial was held on Friday, April 6, in Enugu.
