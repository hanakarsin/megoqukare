Olarinoye Wins First Gold Medal For Nigeria

Jul 15, 2024 Weightlifter, Adijat Adenike Olarinonye, has won the first gold medal for Nigeria at the ongoing Commonwealth Games in Birmingham. Olarinoye recorded a total of 203kg in the women’s 55kg weightlifting event, which is the new Commonwealth Games record. Naija News reports that Nigeria currently sits ninth on the medals table with Australia leading the charts with 13 gold, eight silver and 11 bronze. Team Nigeria will hope to add to the one medal haul when actions resume tomorrow on Day 3 of the Commonwealth Games.
