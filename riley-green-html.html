Riley Green - Age, Family, Bio

Country music singer and guitarist who has won fans across the United States with songs like "Bury Me in Dixie," "Georgia Time," and "Chasin' This Drunk." He is also known to CMT viewers for being crowned the winner of the reality show Redneck Island. 

Before Fame

He was a 3-sport athlete in high school and a quarterback at Jacksonville State University.

Trivia

He starred on his own hunting show on Dirt Road TV called In The Hunt.

Family Life

He credits his grandfather, Buford Green, with inspiring his love of music. 

Associated With

He has covered the Jamey Johnson song "Can't Cash My Checks."
