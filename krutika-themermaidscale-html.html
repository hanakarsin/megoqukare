Krutika TheMermaidScale - Age, Family, Bio

TikTok content creator known for sharing various skits and lip sync performances on her verified themermaidscale account. Her popularity on the platform has led to her amassing more than 16 million fans. She has also posted comedic short videos on her YouTube channel, like her "Remove the third letter of your name for a game #shorts" video. 

Before Fame

She began her career as a content creator by posting fashion videos on YouTube. In 2017, she shared a fall look book on her channel.

Trivia

She enjoys watching anime and Korean drama programs on television. In October of 2021, she posted a reel on Instagram about singing in the shower. The video has since received over 2 million hearts.

Family Life

She was born in India. She has a sister.

Associated With

She created a duet video featuring Emma White that parodied a Britney Spears concert.
