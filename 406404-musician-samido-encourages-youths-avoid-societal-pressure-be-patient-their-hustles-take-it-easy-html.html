Musician Samido Encourages Youths to Avoid Societal Pressure, Be Patient with Their Hustles: &quot;Take I

- Samidoh asked the youths to avoid rushing their life and be patient with their dreams of making it in life

- The musician said that the goal is for people to actually succeed and not to look successful

- His fans agreed with him, with some wishing him all the best as he embarks on taking up a show in Nakuru

Popular Kikuyu musician Samidoh has encouraged the youth to be patient with their day-day-hustle and avoid societal pressure.

The singer shared the inspirational post on his social media page after a meeting with one of his fans who drives a TukTuk.

He shared a lengthy post just advising young people to avoid rushing their life just because they want to look successful, but instead urged them to practice patience.

Part of his encouraging message read:

''Many youths are under a lot of pressure these days. Sadly, it is not the pressure to achieve but the pressure to look like they are achieving. Take it easy, avoid competition. The goal is to be successful and not to look successful.''

Samidoh also gave a shout-out to the young fan and urged people to normalise paying the driver after using his services.

''Bumped into one of my fans who has pimped his low flight heavy machine with my photos. He carried me from Githurai 45. Please guys, pay him anytime you board, he says you guys refuse to pay anytime you see my pictures,'' he added.
