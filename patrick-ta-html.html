Patrick Ta - Age, Family, Bio

Social star and makeup artist known for his work on stars from Joan Smalls to Kourtney Kardashian. Known for his signature glamazon looks, he has gained a significant Snapchat following as well as more than 3.5 million followers on Instagram. 

Before Fame

While living in Arizona, his roommate was a makeup artist at MAC. It was through her that he too began working as a MAC artist.

Trivia

He was featured in a Vanity Fair article in September 2017. 

Family Life

Notoriously private, he was born in San Diego and later lived in Los Angeles. 

Associated With

His Instagram account features a wide range of photos with clients including Shay Mitchell, Gigi Hadid, and Chrissy Teigen.
