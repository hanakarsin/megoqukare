Ondo Deputy Gov Continues As Acting Gov As Akeredolu Extends Medical Leave

Jan 1, 1 Ondo State Governor, Rotimi Akeredolu, has reached out to the state House of Assembly to extend his medical leave. In his letter, he noted that his request was based on doctors’ advice, suggesting he needed more rest after his recovery. The Speaker of the Assembly, Rt Hon Olamide Oladiji, confirmed the receipt of the letter in Akure, the state capital. Oladiji applauded Akeredolu for adhering to the Constitution of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, particularly section 190, expressing gratitude for his speedy recovery and expressing hope for his swift return.
