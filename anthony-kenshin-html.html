Anthony Kenshin - Trivia, Family, Bio

Family member who is best known for being the younger brother of famed YouTuber CoryxKenshin. He also pursued a content creating career of his own. Despite a poor financial situation, the men have grown to become especially close.

Before Fame

He made his first appearance on his brother's channel in the 2010 video titled, "MY KINGS ISLAND ADVENTURE!!!!" He was approximately between the ages of five and seven when he made this appearance.

Trivia

He reportedly did not accept financial help in pursuing his goals to become a video content creator like his brother. Both of them were covered in bug bites from head to toe following the video's recording and release. He has also been known to go by the nickname "Certified AK."

Family Life

He has an older brother known throughout the world as Cory X Kenshin. 

Associated With
