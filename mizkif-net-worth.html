Mizkif, otherwise known as Matthew Rinaudo, boomed into popularity in 2018 and has amassed a loyal Twitch following. The streamer has been a juggernaut on the platform, standing on the upper crust with Kai Cenat and Jynxzi.

Although, Mizkif has encountered his fair share of controversy and was the subject of serious allegations. Despite the blemishes on his image, the streamer has always been a top earner among his peers based on revenue leaks.

As you might expect, Mizkif’s net worth has soared through the ups and downs, but a recent leak of his earnings has viewers claiming the streamer is making less revenue than before.

What is Mizkif’s net worth in 2023?

So far in 2023, Mizkif’s net worth is estimated to sit between $1.5 to $5 million, but that figure could change depending on his streaming performance going into the end of the year.

He also has a YouTube channel with over 900,000 subscribers that averages over 100,000 views and will contribute toward his overall revenue. On Twitch, Mizkif currently has two million followers and over 6,000 subscribers.

Throughout 2023, Mizkif has been livestreaming less. With this in mind, it’s fair to say those previous figures have dwindled, and fans seem to back up this line of thinking after learning about his recent earnings from the platform.

Mizkif accidentally leaked his Twitch revenue during a livestream.

During a Just Talking stream, Mizkif was shocked by all the viewers commenting, to the point where the broadcast was lagging mid-stream. During this, the streamer accidentally switched the screen to his Twitch account page and leaked the revenue he made from Sept. 5 to 11.
