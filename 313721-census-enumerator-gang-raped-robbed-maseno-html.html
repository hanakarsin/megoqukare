Census enumerator gang raped, robbed in Maseno

- The woman had just been dropped by her supervisor before the incident happened

- Police say attackers were hiding outside the enumerator's gate and pounced on her before she entered her house

- The Statistics Department in Kisumu has been ordered to suspend supervisor due to negligence

- Police and other census officials have been summoned to record statements with the police to help net suspects

- The attackers made a way with the woman's power bank and torch but did not interfere with censors material she was carrying

- Two suspects have been arrested and are helping police in the probe

Police in Kisumu County have launched a manhunt for attackers who gang raped and robbed a census enumerator in Maseno.

TUKO.co.ke has learnt that the incident happened on the night of Saturday, August 24 after the woman was dropped at her gate by a supervisor.

The attackers, according to a report filed buy Citizen Digital on Sunday, August 25, were hiding outside the woman's gate and they descended on her immediately she disembarked.

Confirming the incident, Nyanza Regional Commander Vincent Makokha stated that the County Statistic Department had been ordered to suspend the supervisor due to negligence.

The supervisor and police who were deployed in Maseno to beef up security during censor have since been summoned to record statements with police even as investigations intensify.

According to Makokha, the suspects made away with the victim's power bank and torch but did not interfere with census materials she was carrying.

The enumerator is currently receiving medical attention and she is in stable condition.
