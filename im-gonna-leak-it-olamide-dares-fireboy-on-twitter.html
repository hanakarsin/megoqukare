Im Gonna Leak It - Olamide Dares Fireboy On Twitter

Nigerian musician, Olamide Gbenga Adedeji, popularly known as Olamide, has caused stirs on Twitter after threatening to release Fireboy’s 3rd studio album.

According to the hip hop star, Fireboy’s 3rd album has been with him since October last year and the youngster has refused to make move for its release.

Olamide who seems to be desperate to release the album told his followers to reach out to Fireboy else he will release it.

The celebrated singer added a bit of humour to his post which has generated reactions, Naija News reports.

Olamide Badoo as he is also known tweeted: “I’ve had Fireboy 3rd album with me since October 2021 and I’m tired of listening to it alone.

“If you have his number, kindly call him n let him know I’m gonna leak it if he no pick next single n let’s drop the album.”

See the tweet below:

I’ve had fireboy 3rd album with me since October 2021 and I’m tired of listening to it alone.. if you have his number, kindly call him n let him know I’m gonna leak it if he no pick next single n let’s drop album 😂

— Olamidé (@Olamide) February 9, 2022

Naija News understands that Olamide my be using the viral Oxlade s3xtape leaked online to catch some cruise.

A series of reactions have, however, followed Olamide’s post on the microblogging platform.

Popular TL commenter, Daniel Regha [@DanielRegha] opened the flow with his epistle saying: “Olamide it’s better u don’t release Fireboy’s album now so songs on the album don’t overshadow his Peru remix with Ed Sheeran; Also music isn’t something u produce hastily, Fireboy should slow down, up his pen game & explore different music themes so he can evolve as an artiste.”

Commenting with sarcasm, one Ola [@daddyz33m] tweeted: “Olamide in as much as u re fireboys boss&u ve d privilege 2 ve access 2his 3rd album, u shouldn’t come online 2threaten leaking it coz fireboy have d soul right 2d album. Also u need 2 apologize 2d fan who asked 4ur help. Speaking 4 regha since I didn’t see his comment on here.”

Boluwatife [@drealbstar] who seems to understand a bit of Olamide’s post tweeted: “Baddo no say fire citizens dey crave …He just use style use dis post wash hand comot , so we go fit put fire for @fireboydml yansh.

He added: “Issokay ha, But @fireboydml we go manage wait make u finish ur tour …. Anything after dat …Na our tweet go spoil ur phone I come in peace.”

Oluwashinaayomi_14 [@ShinaAyo_14] was not in support of Olamide pressuring Fireboy to release the album and the netizen has this to say; “I would have say, u should Leak it, but sir let peru smash d world abeg, let d album drop in d next 7 month, let peru touch every part of the world 1st abeg, but he can give us new single by next month or April.”

However, desperate Dynamite001 [@yp_dynamite] would not wait any longer to jump on every track on the said music album as he replied his fellow commenter saying: “See you, aren’t you tired of hearing all these songs that these remaining artist are releasing nii?

“Fireboy album is needed more than anything, he should put it inside the album, we need that album, it’s really important”
