Potters Squad For Tuesday Champions League Match Revealed

Premier League side, Chelsea will today be hosted at Red Bull Arena for their UEFA champions league match against RB Salzburg.

Hours before the match kick-off time, Football London revealed that Chelsea manager, Graham Potter has named a formidable first eleven and substitutes to face their Group E stage opponent on Tuesday evening.

The likes of Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang, Kai Havertz and Raheem Sterling were included in the line-up to tackle RB Salzburg, Naija News understands.

Chelsea is currently leading the Group E with seven points ahead of RB Salzburg that have won only one match in the last four games.

See below Chelsea’s Full Squad Against Salzburg:

Goalkeepers: Kepa Arrizabalaga, Edouard Mendy, Marcus Bettinelli.

Defenders: Ben Chilwell, Kalidou Koulibaly (Doubt), Thiago Silva (Doubt), Marc Cucurella, Cesar Azpilicueta, Trevoh Chalobah.

Midfielders: Ruben Loftus-Cheek, Mateo Kovacic, Jorginho, Denis Zakaria, Mason Mount, Conor Gallagher, Carney Chukwuemeka.

Forwards: Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang, Armando Broja, Kai Havertz, Christian Pulisic, Raheem Sterling. The kick-off time for RB Salzburg vs Chelsea match today is 5:45 pm.

Naija News recalls that Chelsea ended in a 1-1 draw with Manchester United in the Premier League in their previous match last weekend. Chelsea is currently sitting in the fifth position on the Premier League table with 21 points from 11 matches.
