Man United Match Against Aston Villa Moved Forward

The English Premier League match between Manchester United and Aston Villa has been rescheduled.

Naija News understands the game scheduled to hold on September 25, was rescheduled to avoid significant crowd, traffic and operational issues locally.

It was reported that a Courteeners concert will hold at Lancashire Cricket Club on the same day with the EPL games.

United versus Villa match which was supposed to be played at Old Trafford at 5pm has now been moved forward to 12.30 pm.

Naija News understands that United vs Villa match clashes with Manchester City vs Aston Villa.

The Red Devils in an official statement on Thursday, appealed to fans of the club for any inconvenience caused.

The statement reads: “This request was supported by Greater Manchester Police and TfGM to avoid significant crowd, traffic and operational issues locally.

“We regret any disruption that this may cause around our supporters’ plans for the day.”

In another news, FIFA placed ban on eight English Premier League players.

Naija News reports that as a result of the ban, star players from Manchester City, Leeds, Liverpool, Chelsea and Manchester United will not participate in this weekend’s matches.
