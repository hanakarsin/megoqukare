Singer Akothee tells scandalous men to chase after her daughters, not her

Controversial Kenyan songstress Akothee has urged all scandalous men who have been on her back trying to woo her to let her be and instead go for her daughters.

The mother of five claimed she is tired of men, specifically fellow celebrities, who have been trying to make all manner of publicity stunts implicating her as their lover.
