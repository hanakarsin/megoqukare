Miki Hamano - Age, Family, Bio

Actress and model picked up by Scout Model Agency San Francisco and Wilhelmina Models Los Angeles. In 2018, she made her motion picture debut in Perfect.

Before Fame

A day after beginning collegiate experience in the United States, a classmate of hers asked her to model for a project. This was the inception for her modeling career.

Trivia

Her modeling experience and Los Angeles lifestyle would be displayed on her misshamino Instagram for more than 220,000 followers.

Family Life

She is originally from Japan and had never been outside her home country until she was 18 years old. She has a sister.

Associated With

She was featured in the movie Perfect which starred Abbie Cornish.
