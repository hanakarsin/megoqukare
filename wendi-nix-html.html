Wendi Nix - Age, Family, Bio

Anchor and sports reporter widely known for representing ESPN in coverage for the NFL and collegiate football games in New England. She got her start as a sports anchor for WHDH in Boston, Massachusetts. She is also well known for covering events like the U.S. Open, Masters, and British Open golf tournaments.

Before Fame

She earned a degree in French and economics from Wofford College and followed with her masters in sports management from the University of Massachusetts Amherst.

Trivia

She has served as a guest analyst on the weekend sports show Sports Extra on WHDH.

Family Life

She married former Boston Red Sox general manager Ben Cherington. The couple announced their split in a 2010 interview in the Myrtle Beach Tribune.

Associated With

Both she and Lisa Salters have served as journalists for ESPN.
