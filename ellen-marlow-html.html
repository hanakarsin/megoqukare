Ellen Marlow - Age, Family, Bio

Former child actress best known for her role in the 2008 movie The Clique. She also appeared in Quid Pro Quo.

Before Fame

She had her first acting experience in a local stage production of The Wizard of Oz.

Trivia

She had guest television roles on CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, Criminal Minds, and Cold Case. 

Family Life

She is very close to her older brother, J.R.

Associated With

She played the young version of Vera Farmiga's character in the 2008 drama Quid Pro Quo.
