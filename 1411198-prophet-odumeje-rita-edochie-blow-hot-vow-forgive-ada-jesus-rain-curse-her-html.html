Prophet Odumeje, Rita Edochie Blow hot, Vow never to Forgive Ada Jesus as They Rain Curses on Her

- Prophet Chukwuemeka Odumeje has refused to forgive Ada Jesus who accused him of performing fake miracles

- The comedian is down with kidney disease and was recently taken to Odumeje's church for forgiveness but the cleric said he would never forgive her

- A Nollywood actress, Rita Edochie, who said the comedian insulted her, also vowed not to forgive her

Prophet Chukwuemeka Odumeje has vowed not to forgive comedian Ada Jesus who is seriously sick after the latter accused him of performing fake miracles and deceiving people.

The young lady, who was diagnosed with kidney disease, was taken to Odumeje's church to ask for forgiveness but the pastor turned down her plea and rained curses on her for speaking against him.

Nollywood actress Rita Edochie also rained curses on the lady whom she said accused her of insulting her family.
