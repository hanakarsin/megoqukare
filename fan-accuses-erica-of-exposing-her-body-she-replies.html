Fan Accuses Erica Of Exposing Her Body, She Replies

Former Big Brother Naija housemate, Erica Nlewedim has given a savage response to a fan who recently accused her of exposing her body.

The fan had taken to social media to troll Erica, accusing her of exposing her body unnecessarily despite being a beautiful woman.

Naija News understands the fan commented based on an endorsement video uploaded on social media by Erica in which she wore a red off-shoulder gown with some of her body parts revealed.

The fan wrote: “You are beautiful already, why the exposure of your prestigious body parts as a woman.”

However, Erica in her response told the fan that maybe she “wanted to share more of the beauty.”

Meanwhile, Nollywood star actress, Tonto Dikeh has added another feather to her cap with her appointment as an ambassador for peacebuilding.

She was given the fresh appointment by the Nigerian Christian Pilgrim Commission (NCPC) on Thursday, 25th February 2021 in Abuja by Rev. Yakubu Pam, the Executive Secretary of the Nigerian Christian Pilgrim Commission (NCPC).

The actress took to her Instagram page on Friday morning to reveal details of the new appointment and also shared pictures of the event.

She ended the statement by asking Nigerians for prayers and support as she begins her new role as a peace ambassador for the Christian pilgrim commission.
