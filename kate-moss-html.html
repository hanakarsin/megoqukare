Kate Moss - Age, Family, Bio

Fashion model who popularized the heroin chic fashion trend of the 1990s. TIME magazine named her one of the 100 most influential people in the world in 2012.

Before Fame

She attended Riddlesdown Collegiate during her high school years.

Trivia

In 2012, she was ranked #2 on Forbes' Highest Paid Models List, with earnings of almost $10,000,000.

Family Life

She married guitarist Jamie Hince on July 1, 2011 and she has a daughter named Lila Grace Moss. The couple later divorced in 2016. She has been romantically linked to Nikolai von Bismarck. 

Associated With

She and Gisele Bundchen often battle it out for the top spot on Forbes' list of Highest Paid Models in the Industry.
