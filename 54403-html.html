Richard Mofe Damijo's son weds

Oghenekome Mofe, son of prominent Nollywood actor, Richard Mofe-Damijo popularly called RMD walked down the aisle with his sweetheart, Eniola Abiodun on Saturday.

The event held at the Chapel of Christ The Light Church, Alausa, Ikeja with the reception following immediately at  Grandeur Event Center, Oregun.

The colourful wedding was graced by celebrities as Teju Babyface, Bovi, Ali baba, Okey Bassi and others.

Also in attendance was the Gov of Delta State and his wife, the wife of the deputy gov of Delta State, members of the Delta State executive counsel and kings.
