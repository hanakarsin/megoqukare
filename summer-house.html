'Summer House' Latest News and Updates

The reality series Summer House is filmed in Montauk and follows a group of friends as they escape to the Hamptons during the hot summer months. The original cast featured Kyle Cooke, Lindsay Hubbard, Carl Radke, Stephen McGree, Lauren and Ashley Wirkus, Everett Cristina Gibson, and Jaclyn Shuman. Over the years, additional cast members have included Amit Neuman, Jordan Verroi, and Jules Daoud.
