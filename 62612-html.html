Newborn baby Abandoned In Soak-Away

Residents of the Surulere community, area of Abeokuta, Ogun state woke up to the non-stop cry of a newborn baby who was dumped in a septic outlet.

The baby who still had the placenta uncut had crying for nearly 24 hours was discovered around 6pm after residents raised a rescue team who in turn found for the baby in the pit.

The councillor representing the area, Ayoola Kabir said that he was contacted at about 6.45 pm, and made arrangements to get the baby to the hospital.

“First, the idea of contacting the Police came, but, I considered saving the life of the innocent baby first.

“From experience, going to the Police may result in losing the baby, so we took him to the Local Government Health centre, and he was fully revived and a jaundice injection was given. I came back this morning, Sunday, and thank God, he’s doing fine.”

Kabir further said the damaged pit slab which had collapsed some years ago but was not replaced was what saved the baby from falling deeper into the soak-away pit.
