Kisii Man Brings Pet Dog to Simba Arati's Meeting, Excites Kenyans

  • A man from Kisii county was pictured with his dog during Dagoretti North MP Simba Arati's political rally
  • He was holding his pet high when the photo which is already circulating on social media platforms was taken
  • Many said while the picture was amusing, the Kisii resident could have been passing a coded message to politicians

A man from Kisii county has treated Kenyans to a moment of laughter after showing up at a political rally with his pet dog.

The man seemed excited to have his dog in Dagoretti North MP Simba Arati's political rally in Daraja Mbili residents, Kisii county.

In the picture shared widely on social media, he held high his pet as the politicians addressed the residents, and this made for a perfect shot.

The Kisii resident's love for his dog amused many Kenyans while others thought he was just seeking to be famous since it is the political season once again:

Social media reactions

Here are some of the reactions:

AlexKula Adhola NyakwarBaki Kingi commented:

"The dog is named in honour of Simba Arati."

Ruto's tour in Kisii

This came just a week after Deputy President William Ruto's tour to Kisii which was marred by violence and bad weather.
