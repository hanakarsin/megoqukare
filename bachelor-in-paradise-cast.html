'Bachelor in Paradise' 2019 Cast Leaked Plus Season 6 Spoilers!

'Bachelor in Paradise' has officially started filming. Here is a look at the yet-to-be revealed 2019 cast — plus spoilers of what to expect during Season 6.

If you are a Bachelor fan, then you know it's not really summer until filming begins on Bachelor in Paradise. Well, the time has officially arrived, as some of your favorite past Bachelor Nation stars are already in Mexico to film the dating reality show south of the border. 

Thanks to Reality Steve, we have a first look at the 2019 Bachelor in Paradise cast (which has yet to be announced by ABC) and we can tell it's going to be a great season. 

How long does Bachelor in Paradise filming take, you ask? 

In general, filming on BIP takes about three weeks, so less about a third of the time it takes to put together a complete season of The Bachelor or Bachelorette, which takes approximately nine weeks. 

Keep reading to find out who is heading to Mexico for Season 6 of Bachelor in Paradise, which premieres on Monday, July 29. Warning: spoilers ahead!

Hannah Godwin

Hannah G. was easily a favorite on Colton Underwood's season of The Bachelor so it's no surprise that she is one of the first to arrive on set of Bachelor in Paradise. 

Rumor had it, she was on the short list to be the next Bachelorette. 

Instagram: @hannahg11

Dylan Barbour

Currently on Hannah B.'s season of The Bachelorette, it is safe to say that Dylan does not get the final rose, but he did get a round-trip ticket to Paradise. 

Hopefully, the 24-year-old tech entrepreneur finds love in the sun. 

Instagram: @dylanbarbour

Tayshia Adams

She made it to the Top 3 on Colton Underwood's season — along with Hannah B. — and now Tayshia is back for another chance of love. 

But are we all going to forget Tayshia had a boyfriend just a few weeks before filming The Bachelor? Shady.

Instagram: @tayshiaaa

Jordan Kimball

Bachelor in Paradise wouldn't be complete without male model Jordan Kimball. One eagle-eyed Bachelor fan spotted the reality star at the airport heading back to Mexico. 

"My family is at the gate at the airport and we ran into Jordan Kimball! He’s on his way to Mexico to film Bachelor in Paradise," the fan wrote on Facebook. 

Hopefully, he didn't forget to pack his gold Speedos. 

Instagram: @jordan_kimball

Demi Burnett

After making a cameo (or two) on Hannah B.'s season of The Bachelorette, it is no surprise that Demi is also headed to Paradise. 

Well, at least we know she will bring the drama. 

Instagram: @demi_not_lovato

Blake Horstmann

Blake first appeared on Becca Kufrin's season of The Bachelorette in 2018 — and now he's making his debut on BIP one year later. 

Instagram: @balockaye.h 

Caelynn Miller-Keyes

To say that Miss North Carolina was blindsided by her elimination on Colton Underwood's season of The Bachelor would be an understatement. 

But seeing as she lives right next door to Colton's girlfriend Cassie Randolph (they literally live in the same apartment building), it's safe to say their breakup is water under the bridge. 

Instagram: @caelynnmillerkeyes

Nicole Lopez-Alvar

Is Nicole the Ashley I? We can probably expect a lot of tears from the Cuban beauty if her time on Colton's season is any indication. 

Instagram: @nicolelovar

Connor Saeli

You probably remember Connor as the guy who left Hannah B. a bunch of Post-It notes around her room following their date. Well, while it didn't work on The Bachelorette, here's hoping the 24-year-old finds his match in Paradise. 

Instagram: @consaelaway

Cam Ayala

ABC. Always. Be. Cam. How long do you think before Cam freestyle raps on Paradise?

Instagram: @camronayala

Onyeka Ehi

Nicole's sworn enemy is also making her way to Mexico for Season 6. What could go wrong? 

Instagram: @onyekaehie

John Paul Jones

The man with three names is coming to Bachelor in Paradise! At least his Instagram handle proves he has a sense of humor about it. 

Instagram: @johnpauljonesjohnpauljones

Sydney Lotuaco

Another alum from Colton Underwood's season, Sydney is the NBA dancer who warned Colton there were ladies on the show who weren't there for the "right reasons" — before he jumped the fence. 

Instagram: @sydneylotuaco

Kristina Schulman

Kristina is officially a veteran of the Bachelor franchise, after appearing on Nick Viall's season in 2017 and Season 4 of Paradise. Here's hoping third time's the charm — and that Deanie Babies is nowhere in sight. 

Instagram: @kristinaschulman

Kevin Fortenberry

After getting injured during a rugby match on Hannah B.'s season, a trip to Mexico is the least Bachelor producers can do for fitness enthusiast Kevin. 

Instagram: @kevinjfortenberry

Bibiana Julian

Another veteran of the Bachelor franchise, Bibiana is going for her third appearance following her time on Arie Luyendyk Jr.'s season of The Bachelor and the short-lived Bachelor Winter Games. 

Instagram: @bibi_julz

Box Joe Barsano

Forget Grocery Joe. This season it is all about the Box King Joe Barsano so step right up ladies. 

Instagram: @theboxking

Plus, Chris Randone and Krystal Nielson are getting married.

Yes, there will be another wedding in Paradise.

"Shouldn’t come as too much of a surprise based on their recent engagement party and meeting in Paradise last year, but Chris Randone and Krystal Nielson will be getting married next week in Mexico as part of filming for “Bachelor in Paradise," Reality Steve tweeted on June 6. 

Instagram: @chrisrandone and @coachkrystal_

Watch Bachelor in Paradise when it premieres Monday, July 29 at 8 p.m. on ABC.
