Steven Porter - Age, Family, Bio

Tattoo artist and reality star known more commonly as "Sketch." He owns the tattoo shop Reppin Ink and starred on the show Tattoo Fixers.

Before Fame

He opened his shop in 2011 and made his debut on Tattoo Fixers in 2015.

Trivia

He's said that he learned how to tattoo by practicing on his dad with a tattoo gun he bought online. He has more than 400,000 followers on Instagram.

Family Life

He has four children, all daughters.

Associated With

He and Alice Perrin both starred on Tattoo Fixers.
