Coco Bliss - Age, Family, Bio

Popular social media star who is best known for her heluvcoco TikTok account where she posts dance videos. Her content has earned her over 2.9 million followers on the video platform. Her videos have often been set to popular hip-hop songs and have included lip-synchs. 

Before Fame

She graduated high school early to travel and work on her career. She launched her TikTok account in March 2020.

Trivia

She had a viral TikTok video set to the song "WAP," which earned over 30 million views. She has accumulated over 280,000 followers on her Cocomfbliss Instagram account. She has posted multiple bikini and outfit pictures on her account. She owns a modeling agency called Bleubaemodels. 

Family Life

She is from Miami, Florida. Her real name is Cholee Land. She has a younger brother. She is Italian.

Associated With

She frequently collaborates with her boyfriend, rapper and TikTok star iLoveMemphis.
