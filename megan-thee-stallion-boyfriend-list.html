The 4-1-1 on Past Beaus

Megan Thee Stallion's Boyfriend List: The 4-1-1 on Her Past Beaus

By Gabrielle Bernardini

May 31 2023, Updated 3:56 p.m. ET

Fans are always interested to learn more about their favorite artists' personal lives — and superstar Megan Thee Stallion is no exception.

While fans of the Houston rapper shouldn't expect her to spill all the tea on her list of exes anytime soon, we're taking a look back at the people she was linked to.

Megan Thee Stallion's boyfriend list: Who has the rapper dated?

Megan Thee Stallion is currently linked to Belgian soccer player Romelu Lukaku, who plays for Inter Milan. The pair sparked dating rumors in May 2023 after being spotted attending his teammate's wedding in Italy.

Megan also may have hinted that Romelu had been in her life as early as 2020, as a song from that year called "Fkn Round" included the lyric, "I got me a European papi out in Italy."

Before this, Megan dated Pardison "Pardi" Fontaine. The musicians have been linked since October 2020, but first sparked rumors in early 2021 after exchanging a few flirty messages on social media. Megan basically confirmed their romance in February 2021 on Instagram Live when she called him "my boo."

Despite breakup rumors along the way, the two continued on, showing their love for each other on social media, with Pardi also standing by Megan during her ordeal with Tory Lanez. But by early 2023, they were seemingly over.

In 2020, fans speculated that Megan and G-Eazy were an item after the two were spotted getting cozy on his Instagram story. In the video, the two are laying on each other on a couch, and the "No Limit" rapper kisses Megan numerous times on the cheek. But Megan shut the rumors down fast: "Lol alright nowww y’all got all y’all jokes out but I am not f--king G-Eazy [sic]," she tweeted.

When a fan asked why G-Eazy was "sucking the makeup off [her] right cheek," she responded, "He like Fenty," referring to Rihanna's Fenty Beauty collection. Case closed.

Back in early 2019, Megan was first linked to Moneybagg Yo. They regularly posted PDA pics of each other on social media. But by December, the exes unfollowed each other on Instagram. The Memphis native told on Beats 1 host Ebro Darden in 2020: “Two strong personalities just bumping heads a lot."

He later added: "It didn’t work but I always wish her the best. I’m really proud of her. The Megan I know and the Megan ya’ll know are two different things. It’s still love, we just didn’t see eye to eye."

The songstress was also linked to another singer in 2019 after a video was circulating online of Hot Girl Meg hanging out with Trey Songz. When it was suggested that the two were more than friends, she tweeted, "Why y’all want me to be a ‘h-e’ so bad?”

She added: “Lol I’ve only dated ONE person y’all know about. All that other s--t is RUMORS [and] LIES ... I ain't addressing s--t else bye."

In June 2019, Megan was also linked to Chris Brown's ex-girlfriend Indyamarie. Page Six reported that the two were together at the the Pretty Little Thing BET launch party. “They’ve been posting thirsty comments lately on social media,” a source told the outlet.

Overall, no matter who the rapper is dating or romantically involved with ... it's no one's damn business.

And that's some real hot girl s--t.
