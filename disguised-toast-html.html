Disguised Toast - Age, Family, Bio

Gaming personality who has earned more than 3.6 million subscribers on YouTube for his videos playing Hearthstone and Among Us. He has posted multiple videos playing with fellow famous gamer, PewDiePie. He also posted his first vlog entitled "2 guys 1 taco" in June 2021. He has called his vlog series "The Taco Vlog". 

Before Fame

He launched his YouTube channel in April of 2015, and published his first video called "Playing Deathwing against his son, Nefarian" shortly after. 

Trivia

His YouTube channel surpassed the 100 million total view milestone in December of 2017. He earned a significant following through his streaming content on Twitch, before announcing he was transitioning his streaming content to Facebook Gaming in 2019. 

Family Life

His real name is Jeremy Wang. He was born in Taiwan and later resided in Canada. He shared a picture with his sister on his Instagram account in January 2018. He dated Twitch star xChocoBars from 2018 to 2020.

Associated With

His "[Hearthstone] STREAMERS REACT TO RAFAAM" video featured Kripparrian and a bunch of other gamers.
