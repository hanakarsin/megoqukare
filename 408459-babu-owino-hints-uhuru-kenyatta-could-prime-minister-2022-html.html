Babu Owino Hints Uhuru Kenyatta Could Be Prime Minister in 2022

- MP Babu Owino took to his social media platforms to give his version of the 2022 line up

- The lawmaker said Raila Odinga would emerge as the president, and his deputy will be Kalonzo

- Babu said Uhuru will take the prime ministerial position and will be deputised by Gideon Moi and Musalia Mudavadi

Embakasi East lawmaker Babu Owino has dismissed the possibility of a coalition between his party leader Raila Odinga and Deputy President William Ruto.

The ODM parliamentarian instead gave his own version of how power will be distributed among the various politicians in the country.

Taking to his Facebook, the MP projected Raila would be president while Wiper leader Kalonzo Musyoka will be his deputy.

He further claimed President Uhuru Kenyatta would be the prime minister deputised by Baringo Senator Gideon Moi and Amani National Congress (ANC) leader Musalia Mudavadi.

In his 2022 line up, Babu said the Speaker of the National Assembly would be Mombasa Governor Hassan Joho while his Senate counterpart will be Interior Cabinet Secretary Fred Matiang'i.

He prophesied he would retain his seat as the Embakasi East MP, and as such, he will also scoop a ministerial position.

If things go according to Babu's plan, Ruto will be left to lick the wounds as Kenya's Opposition leader.

His sentiments sparked mixed reactions, with many dismissing his line up as a mere pipe dream that will never see the light of the day.
