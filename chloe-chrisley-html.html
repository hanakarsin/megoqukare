Chloe Chrisley - Age, Family, Bio

Daughter of Kyle Chrisley and his ex-girlfriend Angela Johnson, who is part of the Chrisley family featured on the reality show Chrisley Knows Best. 

Before Fame

Chrisley Knows Best premiered when she was about one and a half years old.

Trivia

Kyle accused his father and Chloe's grandfather of kidnapping her, but Angela claimed Todd Chrisley was just trying to protect her as Kyle struggles with bipolar disease.

Family Life

Her aunts and uncles are Chase, Lindsie, Grayson and Savannah Chrisley.

Associated With

Her grandparents are Todd and Julie Chrisley.
