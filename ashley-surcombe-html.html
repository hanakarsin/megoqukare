Ashley Mariee Surcombe - Age, Family, Bio

Social media superstar on Twitter and Instagram who is most well known for her AshleyMarieeGaming YouTube channel, which has accumulated over 550,000 subscribers by February 2016. She has been known for her gaming content for games like Minecraft, Among Us and League of Legends. 

Before Fame

She joined the YouTube world on August 6, 2012 and uploaded her first video called "Let's Play: The Eye - Episode 1" on September 10, 2012.

Trivia

She publishes Mod Showcases, Let's Plays, Mini Games, and Hunger Games on her channel. Her Minecraft parody video of a song by Krewella has received over 6.9 million views since it was posted in June of 2014. 

Family Life

She is a twin and has two sisters. She is from Cyprus and has lived in Bude, Cornwall, Harrogate, North Yorkshire, and Cheltenham, England.

Associated With

She is good friends with Team Crafted members Mitch Hughes and JeromeASF.
