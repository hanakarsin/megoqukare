Lola Holderness - Age, Family, Bio

Daughter of communications mogul and YouTube sensation Penn Holderness. She and the rest of her family appear on the YouTube channel The Holderness Family. 

Before Fame

She made her YouTube debut in December 2012 in her father's video titled "Merry Christmas from the Holderness Family!! 2012."

Trivia

The 2013 YouTube video "#XMAS JAMMIES - Merry Christmas from the Holderness Family!" with the rest of her family earned more than 16 million views after it was posted.

Family Life

Her mother, Kim Dean, is business partners with her father. She has a younger brother, Penn Charles.

Associated With

In 2014, she appeared alongside her father in his popular parody song of Sir Mix-a-Lot's "Baby Got Back" called "Baby Got Class -- A back to school parody."
