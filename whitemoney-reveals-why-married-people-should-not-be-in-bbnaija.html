Whitemoney Reveals Why Married People Should Not Be In BBNaija

The winner of the ‘Shine Ya Eye’ edition of the Big Brother Naija show, Whitemoney has listed reasons why married people should be prohibited from participating in the reality show.

Whitemoney in a recent interview with Media Room Hub noted that former housemate in the show, Tega Dominic is still suffering due to her actions while in the house.

He further stated another housemate in the house, Boma is still been dragged because of his romance with Tega while in the house.

Whitemoney added that a lot of people claimed that the ‘Shine Ya Edition’ of the show taught them a lot of things.

“Big Brother, you know I love you so much, you made me a star, anything I asked, you always do for me, if you could do just one thing for me. Scrap out married people from the show, for the sake of the platform. A lot of people said this year edition taught them a lot of things.”

“Married people are humans, not all of them have the strong zeal to wade up a distraction. For instance, Tega is still suffering from her last experience in the show, the same thing with Boma. The fans are still dragging them till today”
