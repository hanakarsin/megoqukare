Nakuru: Midnight funeral leaves Molo mourners cursing

- Joel Chepwony, the Kabianga location chief confirmed a 88-year-old man was buried at midnight at his home in Sachang'wan

- The chief said the old man was buried with a mattress and his blanket as per his will

- Chepkwony noted the deceased had declined to be interred in a casket leaving his family with no choice

Residents of Molo, Nakuru county were left reeling in shock on Friday, October 25, after a 88-year -old man from Sachang’wan estate was buried in the middle of the night.

The incident was confirmed by Joel Chepkwony, the chief, Kabianga location, who said the bizarre decision to bury the deceased at midnight was done by his family according to his will.

Chepkwony spoke to NTV on Friday, October 25.

“As usual, residents of Sachang’wan normally pay their last respects to a dead person during the day, however, in this case, the family chose to fulfill Mzee’s last wishes using his mattress and blanket at midnight in his farm according to his will,” the chief said.
