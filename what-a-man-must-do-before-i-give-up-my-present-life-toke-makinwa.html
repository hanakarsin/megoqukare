What A Man Must Do Before I Give Up My Present Life

Media personality Toke Makinwa has spoken about the state at which her life is now and the possibility of giving it all up for a man.

Taking to her Insta Stories, Toke explained that she had attained a level of comfort and peace that would only be abandoned if a man gifts her the moon.

She explained further that she had worked hard to get to a place where she is now.

She wrote: “At this rate I can definitely say I ve become the knight in shining armour I ‘ve always dreamed of. Feels so good to be so empowered to do for me what I once thought I needed someone to. The bar is set extremely high too,” she wrote.

“Simply put It will take a lot, so much more to impress me cos I ve saved myself so much in this lifetime that to give up my comfort and peace of mind for any man, you ‘ve got to bring me the moon. Bettter still evacuate the earth and give it to me.”
