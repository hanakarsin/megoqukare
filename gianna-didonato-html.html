Gianna DiDonato - Age, Family, Bio

Actress who is widely known for her role as Crystal in the 2016 Netflix revival series Fuller House. She appeared in three episodes during the show's second season.

She made her first on-screen appearance in the 2014 short film Extra/Ordinary as Mara.

Trivia

She has appeared in recurring roles in the TV series The Social Experiment (2015) and Traif: An Unkosher Series (2016).

Family Life

She has been linked romantically to fellow actor Jake Austin Walker.

Associated With

She has appeared alongside Andrea Barber on the Netflix series Fuller House.
