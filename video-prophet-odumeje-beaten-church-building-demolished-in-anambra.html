Prophet Odumeje Beaten, Church Building Demolished In Anambra

The general overseer of the Mountain of Holy Ghost Intervention Deliverance Ministry World Miracle Center, prophet Chukwuemeka Ohanemere, also known as Odumeje, has been beaten up by officials of Anambra state government for obstructing demolition process.

It would be recalled that the ministry located at no 88B Bida Road, Fegge, Onitsha, was marked by the Anambra State government for demolition in April.

The church building is reportedly sitting on a huge canal and obstructing waterways, prompting the government to order its removal as an illegal building in line with Soludo’s plan to demolish over 200 buildings in Onitsha blocking the drainage linking the River Niger.

In the video that has surfaced on social media, government officials were seen hitting the pastor who was later bundled out of the church premises.
