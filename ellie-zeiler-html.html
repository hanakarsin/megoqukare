Ellie Zeiler - Age, Family, Bio

Social media star best known for her elliezeiler TikTok account where she posts fashion and beauty content and lip sync and dance videos. She first gained popularity when Charli D'Amelio commented on one of her TikTok videos about how similar they looked. 

Before Fame

She played basketball and golf through high school. She also directed her school's television program. She began actively posting on TikTok in March 2020. She reached 2 million followers in about a month. 

Trivia

Her videos have earned her over 10 million followers on TikTok. She posts stylish pictures on her Instagram account, which has earned over 1.7 million followers. She often showcases current fashion trends mixed with classic looks. She also posts on YouTube, where she has amassed over 110,000 subscribers.

Family Life

She is from Southern California. Her parents are named Rick and Sarah, and she has a pair of twin brothers named Ben and William. She has two dogs named Teddy and Jules. 

Associated With

In October 2020, she posted a TikTok video where she dances with Alex French and Lyss Antoci to a song by Perry Como & The Fontane Sisters.
