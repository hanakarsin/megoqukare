Aries | Famous Birthdays

Known as the "house of self" and the "sign of the self" due to being the first sign of the Zodiac. The symbol for Aries is a ram, which is based on Chrysomallus, the winged ram that rescued Phrixus and Helle in Greek mythology. The March equinox marks the point when Aries season begins. 

Trivia

Aries originates from the Aries constellation, which is located between Pisces (to the west) and Taurus (to the east). The 1948 film The Sign of the Ram, which stars Susan Peters, was named after the Aries zodiac sign.
