Tiwa Savage Cries Out As Blackmailer Sets To Release Her Sex Tape (Video)

Afrobeat singer, Tiwa Savage has cried out about how she is being extorted over a sex tape.

In a sitdown with Power105, the “somebody son go find me one day” crooner described how she was leaving a radio station and got a message from her road manager insisting she checks her phone for a video.

Tiwa Savage said when she checked her phone she was shocked at the content of the video and revealed that it was a sex tape of her and the person she was dating presently.

Tiwa Savage said she sent the video to her manager who was also in shock and later asked how much the blackmailer was asking for.

When asked if the blackmailer was the person she was dating Tiwa Savage replied that her lover was also in shock and was not aware of the sex video.

Tiwa Savage also added that she was not going to indulge the blackmailer because she knew if she paid him, he would keep asking for more and he might end up releasing the sex videotape.
