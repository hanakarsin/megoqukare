Akothee Questions Who Gifted Her a Husband after Sharing Doctored Photo in Wedding Gown

- A photo of controversial artiste Akothee with an unidentified man is doing rounds on social media

- The mother of five was photoshopped wearing a wedding gown seated next to a man in a black suit

- The furious mother shared the photo, asking who gave her the man, yet she is single

Singer Akothee is furious after a photoshop of her in a wedding gown seated next to a mysterious man in a suit emerged. The mother of five shared the photo, calling out the person behind the photoshop.

Akothee shared the photo, asking who gave her the man, yet she is single.

"Let's move into June with Jesus only. May was too toxic with a useless calendar. The devil was on every page, my God. I am not taking any May chapter to June. Now, who has gifted me a husband? And like we are married already and I am not sure if my conjugal rights were assessed. Who gave me this man?"
