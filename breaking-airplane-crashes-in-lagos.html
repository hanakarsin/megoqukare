Aircraft Crashes In Lagos [Photos]

A small aircraft which cannot easily be identified has reportedly crashed on Oba Akran area of Lagos State and went up inflames.

The aircraft crashed on the busy Oba Akran road close to fuel station and in front of the a bank.

The incident happened adjacent AP Filling Station, and United Bank For Africa, Oba Akran.

According to eyewitness, the incident happened at about 3.30pm.

It was not clear as at press time if the pilots of the single engine airplane survived.

It was gathered that rescue operation by first emergency responders is on-going,

Men of the Lagos Fire and Rescue Service are being awaited for further emergency operation to salvage the situation.
