However, there is a lot that goes into taking care of four kids at the same time who will always need the parents' attention and this realisation was made by none other than renowned media personality Fred Machokaa.

The radio host was stunned when a mother and her quadruplets visited his Isinya ranch for a day out as they celebrated their birthday.

In a video shared on Machokaa's Facebook page, one could spot the four kids being supported by their parents and friends to seat on a table.

Birthday music was playing in the background and people were clapping as the cake was being cut.

One could spot Machokaa, dressed in red and black, with a cap on his head as he joined the family in marking the big day.

In his caption, the journalist said he was yet to recover from the encounter, adding it was a confirmation that God is in charge.

"I am yet to recover from an encounter with a mother of four babies born at the same time! This happened yesterday at Freds Ranch Isinya and it just blew me away! For me, this was more than a miracle from the high heavens, a confirmation that GOD is in charge," he wrote.

He also wondered about some of the challenges the couple face taking care of the four young kids, and urged his fans to pray for them.

"Just imagine the challenge of just this lady (and her husband) carrying these babies, bathing them, dressing them up, FEEDING them....and we are yet to touch on breastfeeding! I request each one of you my good friends to take a moment and pray for this couple.. please," he added.
