Monarch Insurance Kenya contact and other details in 2022

Monarch Insurance Kenya boasts of over 40 years' experience in insurance underwriting in the country. It was established to help meet the rising demand for insurance services in Kenya, on the backdrop of a growing economy.

The company takes pride in its innovative, exclusive, and customer-centred products and services. Its primary goal is to give its consumers the best services and the most affordable insurance premiums. Due to its immense success, the company has opened branches in several towns across the country. If you are looking for a friendly, responsive, and innovative insurance company in the Kenyan market, Monarch Insurance should be one of your considerations.

Monarch Insurance products & services

The Monarch Insurance company is a composite insurer with products in the General insurance sector and the Life insurance segment.

General insurance umbrella

Under this section, the insurance company offers products such as motor and non-motor solutions.

Motor solutions consist of the following:

  • Motor private: It offers cover for the insured's vehicle against financial loss & legal liability arising from accidental damage, fire, theft, and malicious damage.
  • Motor cycle: It covers payments to repair or replace the motorcycle in case of theft or accidental damage. It also caters to the legal liability against injuries to other people and damage to other people's property.
  • Motor private COMESA: The COMESA yellow card policy covers a private motor user against third party risks within the COMESA region. It is a legal necessity before you can cross the border into a foreign state.

    The non-motor solutions comprise of:

  • Machinery breakdown: It is an all-risks product that complements the insurance cover provided by fire insurance. It shields the insured against loss or damage to the insured machinery or unexpected losses that may arise because of machine breakdown.
  • Contactor all risks: It is an all-risks cover that protects the insured against contract works, materials on site, plant, and machinery on site. The policy covers risks such as fire, burglary, floods, accidental damage, and storms. It also covers third party property damage, Third-party injuries, or death caused by the contractor's negligence.
  • Contractor's plants and machinery: This cover offers coverage against accidental damage or loss of machinery and equipment due to fire, water, collision, falls, theft, storms, or malicious destruction.
  • Engineering conloss: It offers coverage against material damage or loss of property used for business. It also safeguards the insured against reduced gross profit because of turnover reduction and extra expenses incurred in minimizing the decline in earnings.
  • Electronic equipment & computers: It safeguards the insured against accidental loss or damage to electronic equipment and computers, loss of income, supplementary costs incurred in the materials' repair and the cost of restoring data lost because of electric equipment breakdown.

    Life insurance umbrella

    Monarch Insurance company provides the following products under life insurance:

    • The Soma Assurance plan: it helps meet the ever-growing need of parents to have their children access better education, and to continue to do so even in the event of the death of the parent.
    • The Likizo Shwari plan: It allows the insured to take a break and cool off the pressure while enjoying a relaxing holiday away from home. It features a customized plan with simple, flexible payment methods to aid you in planning for a holiday with no hassles.
