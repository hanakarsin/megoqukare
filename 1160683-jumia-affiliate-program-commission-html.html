Jumia affiliate program and commission

The huge retail company Jumia has a special affiliate program for helping their customers to make the profit. This is a legitimate way to earn money, and many people have already tried that successfully. In this article, you will learn everything about Jumia's program.

How to join Jumia affiliate program?

Lots of reliable internet bloggers have mentioned that the Jumia affiliate program Nigeria has brought them a good income. Jumia affiliates have a nice chance to increase their budget. You probably think where to start. In this case, you should not worry – this article will tell you exactly about all the benefits of Jumia affiliate marketing and things you need to know if you would like to become a Jumia affiliate.

By the way, Jumia is an international company, which has its branches in many other African countries, including Egypt, Kenya, Morocco, Ivory Coast, Ghana, and Cameron. So, except for Nigerian products, you can easily promote products from these countries as well. You only have to know that the websites from the different countries have different URL addresses. For instance, the Nigerian webpage is https://www.jumia.com.ng/, while the Ghanaian website is https://www.jumia.com.gh/. Keep this in mind while promoting various products.

Jumia affiliate program

If you have a blog and would like to make some profit off it, then Jumia affiliate program is one of the best ways to increase the popularity of your blog. However, the amount of money that you can make depends on a few factors, and these are the traffic of your blog, the blog niche, and commission model for the production that you want to promote. If you are curious how commission model and structure work, we will explain it below.

How to join Jumia affiliate program?

There are a few particular things that you will have to sort out before you apply for the Jumia affiliate program. These things are:

  • Functional blog
  • Functional e-mail address
  • The bank account

Once you have got all of these attributes, you will be ready to proceed further. Follow the instructions below:

  • Go to the website of Jumia affiliate program.
  • Click on the button “become an affiliate”.
  • Then, you will have to fill in the signup form. You will need to select the type of your account (usually individual), enter your name, phone number, e-mail address, a country where you reside, and the website where you want to place your Jumia affiliate links.
  • Once you have filled this information, you will have to read the terms and agreement of the Jumia affiliate program. Read them carefully and decide if you are alright with them.
  • When you have agreed to the terms, you should click the button “start earning”.
  • You will be sent a confirmation link to your e-mail. Log into your e-mail to confirm your address.

Generate Jumia affiliate links: how to do that?

This is a very important step because it is impossible to make income just by copying the bare link from Jumia store to promote the production. You will need to generate the new original affiliate links for the specific production or the category of product that you would like to promote. You also can get the special affiliate banners on your Jumia affiliate dashboard for placing them on your website. These links have the unique codes, which will inform Jumia that the person came to their webpage from your blog. The system tracks the referral and gives you commission according to the commission rate above.

To generate the links, you will have to follow some instructions:

  • First of all, you will need to visit the official Jumia store website and get the link of the product that you would like to promote. For example, if you want to promote some piece of clothing, you have to go to the Jumia site and copy the link to it.
  • Then, you will need to add your affiliate ID to this link. Promoting the link without the ID means that even if you promote it on your blog, you will not be paid. To add the ID, you should follow the further instructions:
    • Sign in to the Jumia affiliate dashboard.
    • On the vertical menu, you should click on “Tools” and then on “Link builder”.
    • In the drop-down “Offer” select the country whose products you want to promote. If you are in Nigeria, you should choose Nigeria.
    • Copy the product URL from the step 1, and paste it in the field “Page URL”.
    • In conclusion, you will need to copy the text that appears in the field “Final URL”, and put it on your blog.
    • Congratulations, you can now add the affiliate links to your blog and promote the products!

    Checking the money, you have earned from Jumia: is it possible?

    Once you start promoting the Jumia production, you will get the income once someone buys the products from your referred link. Of course, you always can check your earnings. You can do it on a regular basis. Here is how to do it:

    • First, sign in to the Jumia affiliate dashboard.
    • Then, choose your country, and you will see the report and your daily, weekly, or monthly progress. You will have a chance to view the chart which shows your amount of clicks, conversions, and commission.

    If you want to see the detailed sales at Jumia affiliate dashboard, you should sign into it, and then go to the vertical menu where you should choose “Reporting”, and after that “Conversions”. Choose the country, the products of which you promote. Then, choose the period (for instance, from September 2017 to March 2018). You will view the number of your sales, along with the conversion dates, the commission that you have earned from them, and the status – it can be pending, rejected, or approved. You will be paid only for those which are approved.

    How does Jumia pay?

    Jumia makes their payments via bank. Your money will be transferred to your bank account by the electronic transferring method. For the successful money transactions, you will have to enter your banking details in Jumia dashboard. Log in to the Jumia affiliate dashboard site, and click on “Payment”, then “Payment Details”. Fill in the form with your country, payment method (choose “bank transfer” if you want the bank payments), account holder, account number, bank name, bank branch, city, and swift.
