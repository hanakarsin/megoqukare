Jul 1, 2024 Lourie Helzer

CL - Age, Family, Bio

South Korean hip hop artist who became a member of 2NE1 alongside Minzy, Sandara Park and Park Bom. The group released the song "Please Don't Go," which was #6 on the Gaon Chart in 2009. 

Before Fame

She signed her first music contract, with YG Entertainment, in 2007. 

Trivia

She released her second solo single, titled "The Baddest Female," in 2013.

Family Life

She lived with her family in South Korea, Japan, and France as a child.

Associated With

She was featured on G-Dragon's 2009 single "The Leaders."
