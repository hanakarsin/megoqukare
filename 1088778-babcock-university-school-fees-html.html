Babcock University school fees, courses, and tuition details for 2024/2025

Babcock University is ideal for Nigerians seeking higher education at Christian colleges. The university is named after American missionary David C. Babcock, who helped bring the Seventh-day Adventist Church to Nigeria in 1914. Babcock University school fees will help you understand how much you will need to pay for a semester or a single course.

Babcock University is owned by the Seventh-day Adventist Church in Nigeria, and its mission is to educate students in knowledge and character so they can contribute to society. It is culturally diverse, with students from all over the country. It has over 7,000 students, over a thousand staff members, hostel accommodations, a cafeteria, and a library.

What is the Babcock University school fees for nursing?

The School of Nursing Sciences offers only one programme. The tuition for a postgraduate course is N375,000 per semester. For undergraduates, the annual fee for 100 levels is N989,449. For 100 - 200 levels is N889,449, and for 300 - 500 levels is N718,807.

What is the Babcock University school fees for computer science?

For postgraduates, the fees per term range from 300,000 to 375,000. For undergraduates, the annual fee is N989,449 (100 levels), N889,449 (100 - 200 levels) and N718,807 (300 - 500 levels).

How much do you pay for the meals at the university?

Meals have different prices. If you choose two meals per day, you must pay N315,000 per year, and if you choose three meals per day, you must pay N472,500 per year.
