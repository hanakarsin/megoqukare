1. Michael Buffer - $25000 to $100000 per match

Michael Buffer is the highest-paid announcer on WWE. He is best known for being one of, if not the, greatest ring announcer for boxing and wrestling matches of all time. On a handful of extremely rare occasions, Buffer has been paid $1 million. Michael is renowned for his signature catchphrase, "Let's Get Ready to Rumble", which kicks off all major events.

Details about his annual salary are not in the limelight, but he pockets an impressive sum from each match. Depending on the match, Buffer earns between $25,000 and $100,000 every time he utters those five famous words.
